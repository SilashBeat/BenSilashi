About

Ben Silashi is a drummer and composer based in New York City. His playing moves between jazz, improvised music, and groove-driven forms, built on deep listening and a physical, full-bodied approach to the instrument.

He performs regularly across the city's listening rooms and clubs, working as a bandleader and as a sideman with a wide circle of collaborators.
